Cold Email Templates

1. Cold Email — Dentist Niche

Body

You're getting solid traffic, but your Google and Facebook ads are competing with the same 3-4 keywords every other practice in [LOCATION] is using. Customization Tips: Research their current Google Ads spend. Mention a specific local competitor if you can. The "easy win" language is low-pressure.

4. Cold Email — HVAC/Roofing Niche
Subject Line

Body
[OWNER_NAME], Most HVAC and roofing companies run the same ad creative and messaging all year. Problem is: homeowner intent shifts dramatically by season. We helped AceHVAC do seasonal optimization—different angles for AC fixes in summer, heat issues in winter, emergency repairs year-round. 5. Follow-Up Email (Universal — After 3 Days)
Subject Line
Re: [ORIGINAL_SUBJECT]
Body
[OWNER_NAME], Just a quick follow-up on my email from [DAY]. Totally understand if you're swamped—inbox overload is real. No pressure at all, but I genuinely think you'd find a 15-minute call worth your time. We've helped 40+ businesses like [PRACTICE_NAME/BUSINESS_TYPE] in [LOCATION] find easy revenue wins. If this is landing at a bad time, just let me know and I'll circle back in a month. Otherwise, [CALENDAR_LINK] is easiest. [YOUR_NAME]
Best Practices: Keep it super short. Acknowledge their busy inbox. Provide a calendar link (Calendly, etc.). Don't send more than 2-3 follow-ups total.

Outreach Strategy & Sequencing

Ideal Sequence (7-10 day campaign):

  1. Day 1: LinkedIn connection request (use connection message template)
  2. Day 2: If connected, send first DM (value-first angle)
  3. Day 3: Cold email (niche-specific template)
  4. Day 6: Follow-up email if no reply
  5. Day 10: Last-touch DM before moving on

Pro Tips:

  • Personalize every message—mention a specific detail about their business (recent post, client type, location).
  • Keep subject lines under 50 characters. No ALL CAPS unless it's a question.
  • Always lead with a result (number + timeframe: "47 new patients in 30 days").
  • Never say "I want to help"—instead say what you've done for similar clients.
  • Use [PLACEHOLDER] tags to remind yourself what to customize before sending.
  • Send emails Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM - 2 PM (peak open times for busy owners).
  • Track replies in a spreadsheet; if no reply after 3 emails, move to next prospect.

Target Metrics: Aim for a 15-20% reply rate, 5-8% meeting rate, 1-2% close rate to $3-5K retainer deals.
