Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How does the AI personalization work? ▼

Our AI analyzes your workout history, fitness level, goals, and preferences to create completely personalized training programs. It continuously learns from your performance data and adjusts your workouts in real-time for optimal results.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼

Yes! There are no long-term contracts or hidden fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time without penalties. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Is there offline access to workouts? ▼

Absolutely! Download your personalized workouts within the app to access them offline. All your completed workouts will sync once you're back online.

Do you offer a free trial? ▼

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. No credit card required. Simply sign up and start your fitness journey today.

What devices are supported? ▼

FitPulse Pro works seamlessly on iOS, Android, and web browsers. Your data syncs across all devices, so you can train anywhere, anytime.
